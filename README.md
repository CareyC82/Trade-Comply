# Trade Comply

Understand China's trade rules for electronics. Facts only.

A free, front-end-only lookup tool that helps foreign compliance officers, supply chain managers, and importers quickly check China’s import/export regulations for consumer electronics. It only cites official regulatory sources — no advice, no interpretation, just facts.

---

## 🔍 What It Does

Enter a **product name** or **HS Code**, and *Trade Comply* instantly returns all applicable Chinese regulatory requirements, grouped into clear categories:

- **CCC** – China Compulsory Certification
- **SRRC** – Radio Transmission Equipment Type Approval
- **Dual-Use** – Export controls for sensitive items & UAVs
- **Encryption** – Commercial encryption import/export controls
- **Medical Device** – NMPA registration requirements
- **Energy Label** – Energy efficiency labeling rules
- **Battery Safety** – Dangerous goods transport regulations
- **Tax Incentives** – Export VAT rebate information

Each compliance card includes an official source link so users can verify the information independently.

---

## 🛠️ Features

- **Dual-Mode Search** – Look up by product name (e.g. "drone") or HS Code (e.g. "8525.89")
- **Export / Import Toggle** – Filter regulations by trade direction
- **60 Product Categories** – Smartphones, wearables, drones, smart home, medical devices, and more
- **Penalty Case Library** – Real enforcement cases from Chinese Customs for risk awareness
- **Knowledge Base** – Structured directory of key Chinese trade regulations
- **Category Index** – Browse 60 products across 12 groups
- **Mobile-First Design** – Works on desktop and phone
- **Disclaimer Embedded** – Every page reminds users: this is not legal advice

---

## 💡 Why *Trade Comply* Is Different

Most HS Code lookup tools stop at “here is your code”. *Trade Comply* starts from the code and answers the next logical question:

> What regulations does this product trigger when moving across China’s border?

It fills the information gap between *classification* and *compliance*.
